Interactive demo

This in-page preview walks you through saving a thought, seeing lightweight category suggestions, and landing in a folder-style layout—similar to the first-run flow in the app. Use it to imagine how quickly you can offload ideas without breaking focus.

Who this is for

This preview is for anyone who loses ideas between tasks—students juggling assignments, founders capturing product sparks, clinicians noting follow-ups, and knowledge workers who want a lighter inbox than a full document system.

  • Students: park lecture takeaways and due-date reminders without opening a heavy notes app.
  • Founders & builders: stash customer quotes, roadmap thoughts, and meeting prompts in one flow.
  • Clinicians & caregivers: jot non-sensitive reminders to revisit later (never use this demo for PHI; the real app is on-device with your own policies).

How to use this demo

  1. Type a short thought in the box—try words like meeting, study, or workout to steer categories.
  2. Tap Save Thought and wait about a second while the mock “loading” state runs.
  3. Review the saved card, then tap one of the three suggested category buttons.
  4. See the folder-style grid highlight where the thought landed alongside other sample folders.
  5. Refresh the page anytime to reset and run through another scenario.

What's simulated vs. the real app

Everything here runs in your browser with simple keyword rules—no account, no cloud sync, and no machine-learning service. Categories are guessed from a small word list (for example, “quiz” nudges toward School) and the delay is only for show.

The real Thought Sweep app on iPhone adds secure sync, richer tagging, voice capture where available, search across your library, and the full visual design you see in marketing screenshots. Treat this page as a behavioral sketch, not a feature-complete build.

Demo FAQ

Does this page store my thoughts?

No server receives your text. Content stays in this tab until you leave or refresh. Do not paste confidential information if you are on a shared computer.

Why do categories differ from what I expect?

Suggestions are driven by lightweight keyword checks in the script, not by the production app's logic. They are meant to illustrate the flow, not predict your final taxonomy.

Where do I get the real app?

Head to the Download page for the App Store link, device requirements, and next steps after install.

Can I use this demo on my phone?

This tour is tuned for desktop-width layouts. Install Thought Sweep on iOS from the Download page for the handheld experience, haptics, and native capture shortcuts.
